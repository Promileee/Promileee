## 🧭 About Me

I am a motivated undergraduate student passionate about the intersection of **systems engineering**, **data analytics**, and **risk management**. My academic journey has been driven by a curiosity for how complex systems behave over time—and how we can make them safer, more efficient, and more durable.

I believe that engineering is not just about building things that work, but about building things that **keep working**. This belief has led me to specialize in **Reliability Engineering**, where I combine statistical modeling, physics of failure, and operational data to predict and enhance system lifecycles.

---

## ⚙️ My Field: Reliability Engineering

Reliability Engineering is the discipline of ensuring that systems, components, and processes perform their intended functions under stated conditions for a specified period. For me, it is the backbone of sustainable engineering.
